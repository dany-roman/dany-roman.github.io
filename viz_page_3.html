Latitude Does Not Affect Regional Cloudiness

Cloudiness refers to the percentage of the sky that is obscured by clouds. However, there is no correlation between latitude and cloudiness. Clouds can affect temperatures by reflecting light and cooling the planet. Regional humidity and pressure can affect the development of clouds and other water vapor-dependent phenomena.
